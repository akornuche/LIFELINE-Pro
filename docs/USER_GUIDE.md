## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. **Visit the Registration Page**
   - Navigate to `https://lifelinepro.com/register`
   - Select your user type (Patient, Doctor, Pharmacy, Hospital)

2. **Complete Registration Form**
   - Enter your email address
   - Create a strong password (min 8 characters, including uppercase, lowercase, number, special character)
   - Provide required information based on user type
   - Accept terms and conditions

3. **Email Verification**
   - Check your email for verification link
   - Click the link to activate your account
   - Login with your credentials

### Logging In

1. Navigate to `https://lifelinepro.com/login`
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Sign In"
4. You'll be redirected to your portal dashboard

## Patient Portal Guide

### Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard displays:
- **Current Package Status**: BASIC / MEDIUM / ADVANCED
- **Subscription Expiry Date**: Days remaining until renewal
- **Quick Actions**: Book consultation, View prescriptions, Find pharmacy
- **Recent Activity**: Latest consultations and medical records
- **Upcoming Appointments**: Scheduled consultations

### Managing Your Profile

**To Update Personal Information:**
1. Click "Profile" in the sidebar
2. Click "Edit Profile"
3. Update your information:
   - Name, contact details
   - Address, emergency contact
   - Medical information (blood group, allergies)
4. Click "Save Changes"

**To Upload ID Documents:**
1. Go to Profile → Documents
2. Click "Upload Document"
3. Select document type (NIN, Passport, Driver's License)
4. Choose file (max 5MB, PDF/JPG/PNG)
5. Click "Upload"

### Booking Consultations

**Step 1: Find a Doctor**
1. Click "Consultations" → "New Consultation"
2. Filter by:
   - Specialty (General Practice, Cardiology, etc.)
   - Location (State, City)
   - Availability (Today, This Week)
   - Rating

**Step 2: Select Time Slot**
1. Choose your preferred doctor
2. View available time slots
3. Select date and time
4. Confirm consultation fee (covered by your package)

**Step 3: Provide Information**
1. Enter chief complaint
2. Describe symptoms
3. Add any relevant notes
4. Upload supporting documents if needed

**Step 4: Confirm Booking**
1. Review consultation details
2. Accept terms
3. Click "Book Consultation"
4. Receive confirmation via email and SMS

### Managing Dependents

**Adding a Dependent (Max 4):**
1. Go to "Dependents" → "Add Dependent"
2. Fill in dependent's information:
   - Full name, date of birth
   - Relationship (spouse, child, parent, sibling)
   - Medical information
   - Emergency contact
3. Upload identification document
4. Click "Add Dependent"

**Viewing Dependent Medical Records:**
1. Go to "Dependents"
2. Click on dependent's name
3. View tabs:
   - Profile
   - Consultations
   - Prescriptions
   - Lab Tests
   - Medical History

### Viewing Prescriptions

1. Click "Prescriptions" in sidebar
2. View list of all prescriptions
3. Filter by:
   - Status (Active, Expired, Dispensed)
   - Date range
   - Pharmacy

**To Get Prescription Filled:**
1. Select prescription
2. Click "Find Pharmacy"
3. Choose nearby pharmacy
4. Click "Send to Pharmacy"
5. Pharmacy will prepare your medication
6. Pick up at pharmacy location

### Subscription Management

**Viewing Package Details:**
1. Go to "Subscription"
2. View your current package:
   - Package type and benefits
   - Monthly/Annual cost
   - Services included
   - Renewal date

**Upgrading Your Package:**
1. Go to "Subscription" → "Upgrade"
2. Compare package features
3. Select new package
4. Review pricing
5. Click "Upgrade Now"
6. Make payment via Paystack/Flutterwave

**Renewing Subscription:**
1. Go to "Subscription" → "Renew"
2. Review renewal amount
3. Choose payment method
4. Complete payment
5. Receive confirmation

### Payment History

1. Click "Payments" in sidebar
2. View all transactions:
   - Subscription payments
   - Consultation fees
   - Prescription payments
   - Lab test fees
3. Filter by date range, type, status
4. Download receipts (PDF)

## Doctor Portal Guide

### Dashboard Overview

- **Today's Appointments**: Consultations scheduled for today
- **Pending Consultations**: Awaiting your action
- **Monthly Statistics**: Patients seen, revenue, ratings
- **Quick Actions**: Start consultation, View patients, Update availability

### Managing Consultations

**Viewing Appointments:**
1. Go to "Consultations"
2. View calendar or list view
3. Filter by status: Scheduled, Completed, Cancelled

**Starting a Consultation:**
1. Click on scheduled appointment
2. Click "Start Consultation"
3. Record vital signs (if available)
4. Document:
   - Chief complaint
   - History of present illness
   - Physical examination
   - Diagnosis
   - Treatment plan
5. Create prescription if needed
6. Order lab tests if required
7. Schedule follow-up if necessary
8. Click "Complete Consultation"

### Creating Prescriptions

1. During consultation, click "Create Prescription"
2. Add medications:
   - Drug name (search from database)
   - Dosage
   - Frequency
   - Duration
   - Instructions
3. Review drug interactions
4. Add notes for pharmacist
5. Click "Save and Send"
6. Patient and pharmacy receive notification

### Ordering Lab Tests

1. During consultation, click "Order Lab Test"
2. Select tests:
   - Blood tests (CBC, Lipid profile, etc.)
   - Imaging (X-ray, Ultrasound, CT, MRI)
   - Other tests
3. Add clinical notes
4. Select preferred lab/hospital
5. Click "Submit Order"

### Managing Availability

1. Go to "Settings" → "Availability"
2. Set working days
3. Set working hours for each day
4. Block specific dates (vacation, holidays)
5. Set consultation duration (15, 30, 60 minutes)
6. Click "Save Schedule"

### Viewing Payments

1. Go to "Payments"
2. View monthly statements
3. See breakdown:
   - Consultations conducted
   - Amount per consultation
   - Total earnings
   - Deductions (if any)
4. Download statement PDF
5. Submit for approval if required

## Pharmacy Portal Guide

### Dashboard Overview

- **Pending Prescriptions**: New prescriptions to process
- **Ready for Pickup**: Prepared medications
- **Today's Dispensing**: Medications dispensed today
- **Inventory Alerts**: Low stock items

### Processing Prescriptions

**Step 1: Receive Prescription**
1. New prescriptions appear in "Pending" tab
2. Click on prescription to view details
3. Verify patient information
4. Check medication orders

**Step 2: Check Inventory**
1. System automatically checks stock
2. Items in stock: Green indicator
3. Items low/out of stock: Red indicator
4. Click "Request Restock" if needed

**Step 3: Prepare Medication**
1. Click "Start Preparation"
2. Gather medications
3. Package properly
4. Label with patient name and instructions
5. Click "Mark as Ready"

**Step 4: Notify Patient**
1. Patient receives SMS/email notification
2. Patient comes to collect
3. Verify patient identity
4. Click "Dispense"
5. Enter pickup confirmation code
6. Complete transaction

### Inventory Management

1. Go to "Inventory"
2. View all medications
3. Track:
   - Stock levels
   - Expiry dates
   - Usage rates
4. Set reorder points
5. Generate inventory reports

### Payment Processing

1. Go to "Payments"
2. View monthly statements
3. See prescriptions dispensed
4. Total revenue
5. Download reports
6. Submit for approval

## Hospital Portal Guide

### Dashboard Overview

- **Bed Occupancy**: Available vs Occupied
- **Pending Surgeries**: Scheduled procedures
- **Emergency Cases**: Active emergency patients
- **Department Statistics**: Per-department metrics

### Managing Bed Capacity

1. Go to "Beds"
2. View bed allocation:
   - General wards
   - Private rooms
   - ICU
   - Emergency
3. Update bed status:
   - Available
   - Occupied
   - Under Maintenance
   - Reserved

### Surgery Management

**Receiving Surgery Requests:**
1. Requests appear in "Surgeries" → "Pending"
2. Click to view details
3. Review:
   - Patient information
   - Surgery type (Minor/Major)
   - Urgency level
   - Package coverage

**Scheduling Surgery:**
1. Click "Schedule"
2. Select:
   - Operating theater
   - Date and time
   - Surgeon
   - Anesthesiologist
   - Required equipment
3. Add pre-operative instructions
4. Click "Confirm Schedule"
5. Patient receives notification

**Recording Surgery Details:**
1. After surgery, click "Update"
2. Record:
   - Actual start and end time
   - Procedure performed
   - Anesthesia used
   - Complications (if any)
   - Post-operative instructions
3. Click "Complete"

### Managing Admissions

1. Go to "Admissions"
2. Record new admission:
   - Patient details
   - Admission reason
   - Bed assignment
   - Attending physician
3. Update daily notes
4. Discharge when ready

## FAQ

### For Patients

**Q: How do I change my package?**
A: Go to Subscription → Upgrade, select new package, and complete payment.

**Q: Can I add my spouse and children?**
A: Yes, you can add up to 4 dependents through the Dependents page.

**Q: What if I miss a payment?**
A: You have a 7-day grace period. After that, your account will be suspended until payment is made.

**Q: How do I get a refund?**
A: Contact support at support@lifelinepro.com with your request.

### For Providers

**Q: How long does verification take?**
A: Typically 2-3 business days after submitting all required documents.

**Q: When do I receive payment?**
A: Payments are processed monthly, typically by the 5th of the following month.

**Q: Can I work with multiple health platforms?**
A: Yes, LifeLine Pro does not require exclusivity.

## Troubleshooting

### Cannot Login

1. Verify email and password are correct
2. Check if account is verified (check email)
3. Try password reset
4. Clear browser cache
5. Contact support if issue persists

### Payment Failed

1. Check card details are correct
2. Ensure sufficient funds
3. Try different payment method
4. Contact your bank
5. Contact our support

### Cannot Book Consultation

1. Check if subscription is active
2. Verify package covers service
3. Ensure dependent is added properly
4. Try different time slot
5. Contact support if issue persists
